About Us

J Music is a provider of audio streaming services. The company provides offline and online streaming access to music and podcasts catalog and also a music listening experience without commercial breaks. Its major subscription pricing plans include Family Plan, Student Plan and Duo Plan.

J Music also offers audio, display, and video advertising across its podcast and music content. The company sells its premium services directly to end users and through partners such as telecommunications companies who collect payment from the end user for the stand-alone subscriptions. It has business presence across Asia, Africa, Europe, North America and Latin America. J Music is headquartered in Luxembourg.

With J Music, it's easy to find the right music or podcast for every moment on your phone, your computer, your tablet and more.

There are millions of tracks and episodes on J Music. So whether you're behind the wheel, working out, partying or relaxing, the right music or podcast is always at your fingertips. Choose what you want to listen to, or let J Music surprise you.

J Music's platform revolutionized music listening forever when we launched in 2008. Our move into podcasting brought innovation and a new generation of listeners to the medium, and in 2022 we entered the next audio market primed for growth with the addition of audiobooks

Today, more listeners than ever can discover, manage and enjoy over 100 million tracks, 5 million podcast titles, and 350,000 audiobooks on Spotify. We are the world's most popular audio streaming subscription service with more than 551 million users, including 220 million subscribers in more than 180 markets.
